About me

I am a graduate student advised by Dr. Yang Wang in the Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University. My research interests are in distributed systems and heterogeneous architectures. I received my B.S. and M.S. in School of Computer Science and Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.
