Short Bio

I enjoy building systems and my research interests lie in the area of large-scale distributed system with a focus on consistency and scalability. Previously, I was working on high performance computing with a focus on optimizing communication in GPU accelerated clusters.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from The Ohio State University in 2018 and my advisor was Professor Yang Wang. Before that, I received my B.S. and M.S. in School of Computer Science and Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.
